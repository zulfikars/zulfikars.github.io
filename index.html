Muhammad Zulfikar Rakhmat's Personal Homepage

In the name of God the most gracious and the most merciful.

My name is Muhammad. I am an independent journalist, researcher, and disability advocate based in Qatar and the UK. My areas of research concentrate on Asia-Middle East relations, Muslim affairs, and disability rights.

I am originally from Indonesia, but I have lived in the Middle East for the past eight years and have traveled extensively across the region.

My ongoing research: Indonesia-Israel relations, counter-terrorism policies in the UK and Indonesia, and the ongoing conflict in Central African Republic.

I hold a B.A. in International Affairs, concentrating on Middle East and Islamic politics, from Qatar University and am currently pursuing an M.A. in International Politics at the University of Manchester in the UK.

I am a disabled. I cannot use my hands to perform certain actions including handwriting. I have been interviewed by several media both in English and Bahasa Indonesia about my life as a disabled. I have also penned my life story here.

I am also interested in disability-related research, especially Islam and Disability. I have written articles about it and I have been invited to speak about the subject.

I have also a deep interest in computer science, especially open source software and cyber security. On a daily basis, I use Debian and sometimes OpenBSD as my Operating Systems. I am an avid Mac user too. In my spare time, I am passionate to learn different programming languages including C, Python, and Pascal.

I also love to travel. I have traveled to most parts of Asia, Middle East, Europe, and I am looking forward seeing more.

I am available for commissioned and sometimes voluntary works. You can reach me at muhzulfikar [at] gmail [dot] com. I am also on few social media sites including LinkedIn and Instagram.
