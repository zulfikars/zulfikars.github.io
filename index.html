Muhammad Zulfikar Rakhmat

My name is Muhammad. I am a freelance journalist and researcher based in the UK. I have lived in the Middle East for eight years and have traveled extensively across the region. I was born with a disability on both my hands and my speech. Following my graduation, I was interviewed by Doha News and several media outlets in Indonesia about my life as a disabled.


My research focuses on China-Middle East relations, Indonesia-Gulf ties, Muslim affairs, and disability rights. I hold a B.A. in International Affairs from Qatar University and I am currently pursuing an M.A. in International Politics from the University of Manchester.


I am available for commissions, sometimes voluntary works. You can reach me at muhzulfikar@gmail.com.
