Muhammad Zulfikar Rakhmat

My name is Muhammad Zulfikar. I am an independent journalist and researcher based in Qatar and the UK. My research focuses on China's foreign policy in the Middle East, Indonesia-Middle East relations, Muslim affairs, and disability rights. My works have been published in several publications around the world.


I have lived in the Middle East for the past eight years and have traveled extensively across the region. I hold a B.A. in International Affairs from Qatar University and I am currently pursuing an M.A. in International Politics at the University of Manchester in the UK.


I am a disabled. My story can be read in English here and in Bahasa Indonesia here and here.


I am available for commissions and sometimes voluntary works. You can reach me at muhzulfikar [at] gmail [dot] com.
